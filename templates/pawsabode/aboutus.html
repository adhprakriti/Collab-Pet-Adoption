Welcome to PawsAbode!

At PawsAbode, we believe that every dog deserves a loving home. Founded in 2024, we are a passionate team based in Nepal dedicated to connecting homeless dogs with caring families.

Our Mission:

Our mission is simple yet profound: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need. We strive to create a world where every dog has a warm bed, a full belly, and a wagging tail.

Why Choose PawsAbode?

  • Compassionate Approach: We treat every dog with the love and respect they deserve. Our team is committed to providing them with the care and attention they need to thrive.
  • Local Expertise: With our deep understanding of the local community and its needs, we are well-equipped to find the perfect match between dogs and adopters.
  • Transparency: We believe in transparency every step of the way. From the adoption process to our financials, we are open and honest with our supporters.
  • Ongoing Support: Our commitment doesn't end once a dog finds their forever home. We offer ongoing support and resources to adopters to ensure a smooth transition for both the dog and their new family.

Our Adoption Process:

  1. Application: Prospective adopters fill out an application form to tell us about their lifestyle, preferences, and expectations.
  2. Meet & Greet: Once we receive an application, we arrange a meet-and-greet session where the adopter can spend time with the dog and see if it's a good fit.
  3. Home Visit: We conduct a home visit to ensure that the environment is suitable for the dog and to address any concerns or questions the adopter may have.
  4. Finalization: If everything checks out, we finalize the adoption process and send the dog off to their new forever home with a wagging tail.

Get Involved:

  • Adopt: If you're ready to welcome a furry friend into your life, consider adopting from PawsAbode. Browse our available dogs and find your perfect match.
  • Foster: Can't commit to adoption but still want to help? Consider fostering a dog in need. Fostering not only saves lives but also provides valuable socialization and rehabilitation for dogs awaiting their forever homes.
  • Donate: Your generous donations help us cover the costs of veterinary care, food, shelter, and more. Every dollar makes a difference in the lives of dogs in need.
  • Volunteer: Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a difference in the lives of dogs in your community. Whether it's walking dogs, helping with events, or spreading the word, there are plenty of ways to get involved.
